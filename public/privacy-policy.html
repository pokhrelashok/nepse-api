Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NEPSE Portfolio ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our services. We rely on Google services to authenticate you and store your data. Our goal is to collect and use only what is necessary to provide your portfolio features and nothing more.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to operate the service:

  • Account information: Your Google account identifier (such as email) to sign you in.
  • Portfolio data you provide: Symbols, quantities, transactions, or notes you enter.
  • Service metadata: Basic usage logs (timestamps, request type, success or error) for reliability and security. We do not log portfolio contents in these logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate and improve the service:

  • Authenticate you via Google Sign-In.
  • Store and retrieve your portfolio data so you can manage it across sessions.
  • Securely back up your data in your own Google Drive.
  • Monitor service performance and prevent abuse.

3. Where and How Your Data Is Stored

  • Google Drive only: Your portfolio data is stored in your Google Drive within our application-specific folder. We do not store your portfolio data on our own servers.
  • Scoped access: We only request the minimum Google Drive scopes needed to create, read, and update the files we create for you. We cannot view or modify other files in your Drive.
  • Backups and retention: Your data stays in your Google Drive until you delete it or revoke access. If you remove our app’s access or delete the files, we no longer retain your portfolio data.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We do not share your portfolio data with third parties.
  • Service providers (e.g., infrastructure or analytics) receive only minimal metadata needed to operate the service and never your portfolio contents.

5. Security

  • Authentication is handled through Google Sign-In.
  • Data is stored in your Google Drive, which benefits from Google’s built-in encryption and access controls.
  • We use HTTPS for data in transit and restrict internal access to operational metadata.

6. Your Choices and Controls

  • You can view, edit, or delete your portfolio data directly within the app.
  • You can delete the app-created files from your Google Drive at any time.
  • You can revoke the app’s Google access tokens through your Google account security settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

The service is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. We will update the "Last updated" date and, when changes are significant, provide notice within the app.

9. Contact

If you have questions or requests, contact us at portfolionepse@gmail.com.
